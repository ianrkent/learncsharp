Group Excercise

Can anyone actually multi-task?

Give each girl a coloured sticky note, andboys a different colour (not pink and blue!! - get a girl to choose)

Get into pairs, each with a timer (on their phone), draw 3 horizontal lines on a page, creating 4 rows

Round 1: time each other writing "Switch Tasking Is A Thief" on the top line,followed by the numbers 1 - 21 on the second line. Write down your time

Round 2: time each other doing the same thing, but writing one letter on the 3rd row and then 1 number on the 4th row.

Round Up: Compare your results. Notice that task switching led to

  • longer time
  • worse quality
  • increased stress
